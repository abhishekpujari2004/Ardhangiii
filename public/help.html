Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Marriage Bureau?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, fill in your details including name, gender, birthdate, religion, community, location, email, and mobile number. Set a secure password and confirm it. Once done, your account will be created and you can log in immediately.

Is my personal information safe on Marriage Bureau?

Yes, your safety is our top priority. We use advanced security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. You have complete control over what information you want to share with other members.

Can I search for specific members?

Absolutely! After logging in, you can browse through our members list. You can view basic information about each member including their name, gender, city, religion, and community. Our clean card-based interface makes it easy to explore profiles.

How do I log in if I forget which email I used?

You can use either your registered email address or mobile number to log in. This makes it convenient if you remember one of these details. Both are unique to your account.

Can I update my profile information?

Currently, profiles are permanent once created. If you need to update information, please contact our support team through the Feedback page with your request.

Is Marriage Bureau completely free?

Yes! Marriage Bureau is completely free to use. Create an account, browse members, and submit feedback at no cost. Our goal is to help genuine people find meaningful connections.

How do I report a fake or fraudulent profile?

If you encounter any suspicious or fraudulent profile, please report it through our Feedback page. Provide us with the necessary details and we'll investigate and take appropriate action.

Can I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team through the Feedback page with your request. We'll process your deletion request within a few business days.

What should I do if I have technical issues?

If you face any technical difficulties, please navigate to our Feedback page and describe your issue in detail. Our technical support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

Is it safe to share personal details with members I meet on the platform?

We advise you to exercise caution when sharing personal information. Take time to know the person better through the platform before sharing sensitive details. Always meet new connections in public places and inform a trusted friend or family member.

Still have questions?

If your question is not answered above, please feel free to contact us through our Feedback page. Our team will be happy to help!
